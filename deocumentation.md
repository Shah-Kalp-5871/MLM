MLM Compensation Plan Document 
1. Overview 
This MLM system provides three types of income for users: 
1. ROI Income (Return on Investment) 
2. Level Income (Referral Network Earnings) 
3. Club Income (Voucher Reward Program) 
Users participate in the platform by purchasing an investment package. 
For explanation purposes, this document uses a base package value of $500. 
2. ROI Income (Return on Investment) 
ROI is generated based on the user's deposit amount.

3. Level Income (Referral Network) 
Users earn commissions from the earnings generated by their referral network, not directly 
from deposits. 
Whenever a user in the network earns ROI income, a percentage of that earning is distributed 
to their uplines according to the level structure.

4. Club Income (Voucher Reward Program) 
Club income is a milestone-based reward program. 
Users receive voucher coupon codes when they achieve specific direct business and team 
business targets. 
Important rules: 
• These vouchers cannot be withdrawn as cash 
• They can only be redeemed within the company's website 
This encourages purchases and reinvestment within the ecosystem.

6. Definitions 
Direct Business 
Direct business refers to the total deposit volume generated from users directly referred by 
a member. 
Example: 
User refers: 
A → $2000 
B → $1500 
C → $1500 
Direct Business = $5000 
Team Business 
Team business refers to the total deposit volume generated by the entire network under a 
user. 
This includes: 
• Direct referrals 
• Their referrals 
• Unlimited depth in the network 
Example structure: 
A → B → C → D 
All deposits from B, C, and D are counted in A's team business.
