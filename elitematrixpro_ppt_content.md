## 🖼️ SLIDE 6 — DIRECT & TEAM BUSINESS VOLUME

### Section Label
> BUSINESS VOLUME METRICS

### Headline
> **Your Business Grows in Two Dimensions**

### Body
Every user has **two business volumes** that determine their club level and eligibility for level income:

**📌 Direct Business**
> The total investment amount from people you **directly referred** (your Level 1 downlines only).
```
Example: You refer A ($2000) + B ($1500) + C ($1500)
Direct Business = $5,000
```

**📌 Team Business**
> The **total investment** from ALL downlines across all 15 levels.
```
Example: A → B → C → D (all invest $500)
Your Team Business = $1,500
```

### Why Does It Matter?
> Both Direct Business and Team Business together determine:
> 1. Your **Club Level** (unlock voucher rewards)
> 2. Your eligibility for certain **level commission tiers**

### Design Notes
- 2-column layout: Direct Business (left) vs Team Business (right)
- Use tree diagrams showing which users count for each
